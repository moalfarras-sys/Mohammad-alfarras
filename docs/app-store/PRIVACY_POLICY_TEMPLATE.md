# Privacy Policy Template

This template can be adapted for the public privacy page and App Store Connect. It is not legal advice.

## Privacy Policy

MoPlayer Pro is a media player for user-added legal sources. The app does not provide media content.

## Information Stored On Device

The app may store:

- User-added source configuration.
- Xtream username/password or M3U URL.
- Active source selection.
- Favorites.
- Watch history.
- Continue-watching progress.
- App settings.
- Generated device identifier.

Provider credentials are stored locally using platform secure storage and should not be sent through support forms.

## Optional Cloud Functionality

If configured, Supabase may be used for anonymous device registration and non-sensitive sync records such as favorites, watch history, and continue-watching progress. The app does not use Supabase service-role keys in client code.

## Support

When users contact support, they may choose to provide device model, app version, Device ID, and error details. Users should not send provider passwords or unauthorized content details.

## Tracking And Advertising

The app does not implement advertising tracking.

## Deletion

Users can delete local app data from Settings > Wipe Local Data. For cloud-linked records, contact support at https://moalfarras.space/en/support with the Device ID.
