## Review Notes

MoPlayer Pro is a media player only. It does not provide channels, playlists, movies, TV streams, sports broadcasts, premium content, or copyrighted content.

Users must add their own legally obtained M3U/M3U8 playlist or Xtream-compatible source. Provider credentials are stored only on the user's device using secure storage. QR activation is an optional source handoff flow through https://moalfarras.space and does not permanently store provider credentials on the website after import.

To test without copyrighted content:

1. Open the app.
2. Tap "Open Legal Demo" on the sign-in screen.
3. Browse the Legal Demo live category.
4. Open the demo HLS stream.
5. Test play/pause, buffering UI, back navigation, favorites, history, and settings.

To test user-supplied source flows:

1. Open Sign in.
2. Select Xtream or M3U URL.
3. Enter a reviewer-safe legal source.
4. Tap Test, then Save & Enter.
5. Browse Live, Movies, Series, Search, Favorites, Continue Watching, and Settings.

QR activation test:

1. Select Activation.
2. Create a QR code.
3. Open the activation URL on another device.
4. Attach a reviewer-safe legal source.
5. Wait for the app to import it.

Important compliance note:

No App Store screenshots or demo data should show Netflix, beIN, OSN, Apple TV+, paid sports channels, premium movies, protected logos, or any copyrighted broadcast content.
