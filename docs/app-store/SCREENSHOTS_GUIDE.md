# App Store Screenshots Guide

Screenshots must be made from a Mac/iPhone or iOS Simulator after a signed or debug iOS build is available.

## Required Safety Rule

Never show copyrighted or protected content in screenshots:

- No Netflix, beIN, OSN, Apple TV+, Disney, premium movies, paid sports, protected channel logos, or live broadcasts.
- Use only the built-in Legal Demo or another legally owned demo source.
- Prefer screens that show UI, empty states, legal demo labels, and settings rather than real media playback frames.

## Recommended Scenes

1. Welcome/Login with legal disclaimer visible.
2. Add source tabs: Xtream, M3U URL, Activation.
3. Home dashboard with Legal Demo source.
4. Live TV category and slim preview.
5. Movies grid empty state or owned demo content only.
6. Series page empty state or owned demo content only.
7. Player controls over a neutral frame or black paused state.
8. Search and Favorites.
9. Settings with Source Management, Legal, Privacy, and Data Deletion.

## Device Sizes

Capture at least:

- iPhone 6.7-inch display class.
- iPhone 6.1-inch display class.
- Optional iPad only if the app is enabled for iPad. Current project is landscape iPhone-focused.

## Naming

Use safe filenames:

- `01-login-legal-demo.png`
- `02-add-source.png`
- `03-home-demo.png`
- `04-live-preview.png`
- `05-player-controls.png`
- `06-search-favorites.png`
- `07-settings-privacy.png`

Do not commit local screenshot dumps unless they are approved App Store assets and contain no third-party protected content.
