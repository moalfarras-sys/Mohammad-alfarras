# Screenshots Guide — MoPlayer iOS

App Store screenshots must be **clean and legal**: no copyrighted channel logos, no real paid
content, no sports broadcasts, no movie posters, no protected brands. Capture with the
**Legal Demo** source (or neutral placeholder data) only.

## Required sizes (App Store Connect)
- **6.9" / 6.7"** iPhone (e.g. 1290×2796 or 1320×2868) — **required**
- **6.5"** iPhone (1242×2688) — recommended fallback
- iPad shots only if you ship an iPad-optimized build

Capture on the iOS Simulator (Device → matching model) or a real device, then
`⌘S` / screenshot. Keep status bar clean (Simulator: `xcrun simctl status_bar … override`).

## Scene checklist (8 shots)
1. **Welcome / Home** — overview, brand, premium dark look.
2. **Add source** — Xtream / M3U / QR options (the legitimacy story).
3. **Live TV categories** — demo categories list (no real channel logos).
4. **Movies grid** — neutral/demo posters only.
5. **Series page** — seasons/episodes layout.
6. **Player screen** — playing the legal HLS demo, controls visible.
7. **Search / Favorites** — power-user features.
8. **Settings + legal disclaimer** — shows the player-only notice (reinforces compliance).

## Caption ideas (compliant)
- "Add your own legal source"
- "Live, Movies & Series, organized"
- "Smooth HLS playback"
- "Favorites & Continue Watching"
- "Private by design"
- "A media player — you bring the source"

## Hard rules
- ❌ No Netflix/beIN/OSN/Apple TV+/sports/premium movie content or logos.
- ❌ No text like "free channels", "free sports", "watch paid TV".
- ✅ Demo/neutral content only; the disclaimer screenshot is your friend.
