Subtitle suggestions:

- Your private media player

Short promotional text:

MoPlayer Pro is a private media player for legally obtained M3U and Xtream sources. Add your own source, browse your library, and continue watching across a polished iPhone experience.

Long description:

MoPlayer Pro is a media player for users who already have a legal M3U playlist or Xtream-compatible source. The app does not provide channels, playlists, movies, TV streams, sports, or copyrighted content. You add your own source, and MoPlayer Pro helps you browse, search, play, favorite, and resume your media in a premium iPhone interface.

Features:

- Add your own Xtream-compatible source.
- Add your own M3U or M3U8 playlist URL.
- Optional QR activation flow through moalfarras.space.
- Live TV categories and channel list.
- Movies, series, seasons, and episodes when supported by the user's source.
- Search across available content.
- Favorites, history, and continue watching.
- Landscape video playback with buffering, retry, and resume support.
- Local secure storage for provider credentials.
- Clear privacy and data deletion controls.

Compliance statement:

MoPlayer Pro is a media player only. It does not provide, sell, distribute, promote, or bundle channels, playlists, movies, TV streams, premium sports, or copyrighted content. Users are responsible for adding only sources they are legally allowed to access.
