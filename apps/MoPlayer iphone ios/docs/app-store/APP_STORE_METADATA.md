# App Store Metadata — MoPlayer iOS

Copy/paste into App Store Connect. Language is deliberately compliant: MoPlayer is a
**media player only**. No "free TV", "free sports", "watch paid channels", or any wording
that implies bundled or copyrighted content.

## App name
**MoPlayer** (or **MoPlayer Pro** if available)

## Subtitle (≤30 chars) — pick one
- `Your media player for IPTV`
- `Player for M3U & Xtream`
- `A clean player for your sources`

## Promotional text (≤170 chars)
`A premium, privacy-friendly media player for the M3U and Xtream sources you are legally allowed to use. No content is included — you add your own source.`

## Description
```
MoPlayer is a premium media player for iPhone. It plays the M3U and Xtream
sources that YOU own or are legally authorized to access.

MoPlayer does not include, sell, or provide any channels, playlists, movies,
TV streams, or copyrighted content. You add your own legal source.

FEATURES
• Add a source via Xtream login, M3U URL, or QR activation
• Organized Live, Movies, and Series browsing with seasons and episodes
• Fast search across your source
• Favorites, Continue Watching, and history
• Smooth HLS / MPEG-TS playback with landscape controls, buffering state,
  retry, and resume
• Manage and remove sources at any time
• Private by design: your source details are stored securely on your device
• Built-in legal demo source so you can try the interface immediately

MoPlayer is a player and user interface only. It is not a content provider.
```
