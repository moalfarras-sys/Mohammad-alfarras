## Terms Of Use

MoPlayer Pro is a media player application. It does not provide, sell, distribute, promote, host, or bundle any channels, playlists, movies, TV streams, sports broadcasts, or copyrighted content.

Users are responsible for:

- Adding only M3U, M3U8, HLS, or Xtream-compatible sources they are legally allowed to access.
- Complying with all applicable copyright, broadcasting, and licensing laws.
- Keeping their provider credentials private.
- Removing any source they are not authorized to use.

MoPlayer Pro may store source credentials locally on the user's device so the app can connect to the user-added source. Optional cloud sync may store non-sensitive app records such as favorites, history, and continue-watching state for app functionality.

The owner may refuse support for illegal, infringing, abusive, or unsafe use.

## Data Deletion

Users can remove local app data from Settings > Wipe Local Data. For cloud-linked support records, contact support through https://moalfarras.space/en/support and include the app Device ID.
