# Privacy Policy — MoPlayer (template)

> The live policy used for App Store Connect is **https://moalfarras.space/en/privacy**.
> This template documents what the app actually does so the live page stays accurate.

_Last updated: 2026-06-18_

## Summary
MoPlayer is a media player. It is designed to collect as little as possible. Your source
details stay on your device.

## What stays on your device
- Source URLs and Xtream credentials — stored in the iOS secure keychain (`flutter_secure_storage`).
- Favorites, watch history, resume positions, and cache — stored locally (Hive / preferences).

These are **not** transmitted to the developer.

## What is processed for functionality
- An **anonymous, device-scoped** session (Supabase) for optional configuration sync and
  activation. It does not require your name, email, or a password and is not used to identify you.
- A device identifier used only to associate that anonymous session/activation with this device.

## What we do NOT do
- No advertising, no advertising identifier, no third-party ad SDKs.
- No cross-app/website tracking.
- No selling of data.
- No access to contacts, photos, location, microphone, or camera.

## Streams and content
MoPlayer plays sources **you** add. The streams you load are delivered by **your** provider,
governed by that provider's own terms and privacy practices. MoPlayer does not provide content.

## Your controls
- Remove any source and clear local data from **Settings**.
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.
