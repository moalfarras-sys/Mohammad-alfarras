YouTube · My own small orbit

My YouTube world simple, honest and practical

The channel is where I test devices and services in my real life, then turn those experiences into calm, honest videos instead of aggressive ads.

I like the idea that one short video might save someone money, time or frustration.

Why I started the channel

Six short lines that summarize it.

1. I enjoy explaining things in simple language, not in marketing terms.

2. I have tested many devices and services – it felt wrong to keep that to myself.

3. I believe calm content can still be engaging, without shouting or drama.

4. I want the name Moalfarras to stand for something practical and human.

5. I genuinely enjoy filming and editing, even when the setup is small.

6. I think the best thing we can share online is real experience, not perfect images.

What kind of videos?

Reviews of devices I actually use, experiments with subscriptions and tools, and ideas about using tech to support life instead of dominate it.

Common questions

Is everything sponsored?

Most videos come from my own purchases. When there is a collaboration, I state it clearly.

Can I request a review?

You can reach out via the contact page and explain the product and what you want to focus on.

How can I support the channel?

Simple: watch, comment honestly, and share a video that helped you with someone who might need it.
